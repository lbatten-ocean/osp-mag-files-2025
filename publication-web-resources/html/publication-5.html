LAB2MARKET OCEANS is offered in two streams -a 3-day workshop and an 8-week Validate program. L2M Oceans allows re-searchers to test and evaluate the market potential of their in-novations critically. This carefully curated experience is (8 weeks full-time) is complemented by funding, exercises, workshops, and mentorship from industry experts - all with exploration at its core. Eight-week participants will also receive a $15,000 stipend, provided by the Mitacs Business Strategy Internship program.

ocean idea challenge empowers you to kickstart your customer discovery journey, turning your ideas into sustain-able ocean solutions. Compete for up to $7,000 in funding as you validate your market and demonstrate your innovation!

Participants will connect with leading experts and get a strong sense of product-market fit, helping them decide to pursue their idea or pivot.

ocean startup challenge takes your ocean startup to the next level by unlocking resourc-es, funding, expert guidance and networking opportunities.

Participants receive rapid feed-back from industry experts like our executives-in-residence to iterate on their solution and get to market more efficiently. They also receive in-kind support from program partners such as technical support, and co-work-ing space.

We’re making Canada the best place in the world for ocean innovation. We inspire, initiate and invest in Canada’s ocean founders – connecting them with resources, funding, training, and expert guidance to transform their early stage ideas into scalable, sustainable ocean solutions. Our commitment to sustainable oceantech advancements is shaping a dynamic startup ecosystem that bridges local and international realms. boost is a strategic initiative designed to propel the growth of our es-teemed alumni. This exclusive program targets past victors and participants of the Ocean Startup Project, offering a unique opportunity for those actively navigating the journey of commercialization. By provid-ing targeted follow-on funding, Boost bridges gaps and accel-erates progress toward critical milestones.

cdl oceans stream Creative Destruction Lab-Atlantic Oceans stream is located in Hal-ifax, Nova Scotia - the forefront of Canada’s ocean industry. This Stream brings together experi-enced entrepreneurs, investors, and leading scientists from around the world who are active in the ocean industry. CDL start-ups work with these mentors to sharpen objectives, prioritize time and resources, raise cap-ital, and engage with experts working on the frontiers of research.

amplify is a new initiative from the Ocean Startup Project designed to help Canadian ocean startups bridge the gap between innovation and re-al-world adoption. If you’re build-ing a solution for the sustainable ocean economy and ready to accelerate your path to com-mercialization, Amplify gives you the support you need to move faster, toward customers, reve-nue, and impact.
