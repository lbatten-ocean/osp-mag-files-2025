Five years ago, the Ocean Start-up Project set out with a bold vision: to make Canada the best place in the world to start and grow an ocean company. What began as an Atlantic Canadian initiative, powered by partners like Genesis, New Brunswick Innovation Foundation (NBIF), Prince Edward Island BioAlliance,

Springboard Atlantic, Invest Nova Scotia, and Creative Destruction Lab – Atlantic - quickly captured national momentum. As demand grew from innovators and eco-system partners across the country, OSP expanded its reach, welcoming partners in BC and Quebec.

From the start, OSP was more than a project - it was a catalyst. Under Canada’s Ocean Superclu-ster, we set out to support ear-ly stage founders and startups taking their innovative ideas to the market. And we did not do it alone. Collaboration is at the cor-nerstone of everything the OSP does. By working alongside in-dustry, researchers, investors, In-digenous leaders, innovators and organizations, we have helped shape a more diverse and inclu-

sive sustainable ocean economy.

Today, the Ocean Startup Project stands as proof that Canada can lead in ocean innovation. Start-ups in our network have raised significant funding, turning bold ideas into real-world solutions and creating real impact. But the work is not done. We will contin-ue to take action through innova-tion and collaboration. Together, we’ll keep building a connected, ambitious, and thriving ocean economy - one startup at a time.

To every startup founder and team member, partner and col-laborator who’s been part of this journey: thank you. Your passion, creativity and belief in bold ideas have built this vibrant communi-ty and continue to shape a stron-ger, more sustainable future for our oceans.
