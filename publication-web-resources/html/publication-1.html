The Ocean Startup Project is directed and supported by its incredible national partners & collaborators:

The future of our planet de-pends on the ocean, and Canada’s innovators are step-ping up. From restoring marine ecosystems to creating new cli-mate solutions, entrepreneurs across the country are proving that bold ideas can protect the ocean while powering sustain-able growth.

At the Ocean Startup Project (OSP), our mission is clear: find these founders, champion their vision, and give them the resources to succeed. In just five years, we have grown from grassroots Engage Café gather-ings in coastal communities to national Challenges and Ocean-Fest at StartupFest. Along the way, we have supported more than 250 startups, funded 215 directly, and helped them se-

cure over $100 million in com-bined investment, grants, and revenue. Together, they have created more than 330 jobs and are reshaping how Canada en-gages with the ocean economy.

These entrepreneurs are tack-ling critical challenges: captur-ing carbon through ocean alka-linity enhancement, replacing plastics with seaweed-based alternatives, transforming fis heries management with AI, and shrinking the footprint of labs. Their work shows Canadian inno-vation can be both commercially successful and environmentally restorative.

It is an incredible privilege to stand alongside such visionary entrepreneurs, people who are not only building companies but also redefining what it means

to innovate in harmony with our planet.

OSP has also fostered strong partnerships that makes this work possible, with organiza-tions nationwide opening doors to co-working spaces, lab ac-cess, memberships, and techni-cal expertise. This collaborative support network highlights the OSP’s role in building a connect-ed and sustainable ecosystem for ocean innovators.

Now is the moment to dive in. Whether you are a founder, re-searcher, investor, policymaker, or part of a startup in anoth-er sector, the ocean economy holds opportunities waiting to be unlocked. The next wave of ocean innovation is here. Together, let’s make it unstoppa-ble.
